Nice to meet you!

designer.traveller.foodie

Hi! My name is Maria, but everyone calls me Merrie. I'm a visual designer originally from Sofia, Bulgaria, now living in the city of London, studying to become a graphic designer at UAL.

I'm very passionate about healthy living, self-empowerment and enjoying life's twists and turns. Growing up in a smaller country has taught me to always work hard and fight for my dreams, but it has also made me want to strive to bridge the gap between cultures and social groups and support people in need. My ultimate goal is to inspire and help people, as they have done to me.

Apart from design and technology, I have an insatiable desire to travel and explore the world (you can see the many places I've been on my blog and instagram!). I have a love for green matcha lates, avocado toast, spontaneity, and driving through mountains. I can usually be found taking photos, blogging, drawing, reading a good book or trying out a new recipie from Pinterest. I am inspired by people, their culture and their stories, the raw beauty of nature, and the fine line design and technology intersect.
