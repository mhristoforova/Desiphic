SoftUni Digital - Branding and Website

SoftUni Digital is the first complete digital marketing training program in Bulgaria. Developing a visual identity for the platform was a real challenge, as I had to find a way to deliver an experience, which made the user feel at ease with the process of navigating and using all the different features, whilst keeping it very sleek and modern looking.

This work involved user research, user flow, wireframes and visual mocks completed in a bit over a month. The outcome was a logo, printed materials, ads and a complete web platform. The website had to serve both an informational/promotional role, but as well be a training platform with a user profile, homework tracking, courses overview etc.

research

I started my research by interrogating the brand values and researching colour psychology in order to have a better understanding of the brand I was going to be creating. Then I then analyzed the current SoftUni learning platform, as well as other big online learning centers, where I made notes on every step of the experience to get a thorough understanding of how to improve it better.

This helped me...

  • gather a general idea of a colour scheme and common visual language associated with digital marketing.
  • what students' current idea of a learning platform is.
  • figure out what features to target for them.
