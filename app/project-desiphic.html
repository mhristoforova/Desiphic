Desiphic Newspaper

As a part of my degree at UAL, I was asked to design a tabloid newspaper of minimum 4pages on a design subject of my choice. I decided on micro-animations and their relationship to UX, as it's something I'm passionate about and would enjoy reading. It was a big challenge for me, as I'm more used to digital design.

The design process involved a research phase, generating a flat plan, creating content, proofing and printing our final designs. I was aiming for a more modern look, hence the topic, so I managed to find the balance between editorial and newspaper design, keeping some traditional elements from the past.

research

As this was a university task, I was asked to keep track of all my research in a sketchbook, which was a new concept to me. After spending 2 weeks on books, noting down the most important aspects and outlining different article themes, I started developing the final product.

This helped me...

  • understand what makes a newspaper a newspaper
  • discover how can I push the boundries of the format
  • gain knowledge on the subject of motion design
